# The Underreal

## Nature

The Underreal is a plane of maximum Domain expression across all Domains simultaneously — total chaos, no equilibrium. It technically connects all universes, but is not "owned" by any of them.

Because Demiurges are the emergent consciousness of their universe, existing outside that universe is inherently destabilizing. The Underreal, being as far from a stable ordered universe as it is possible to be, is absolute anathema to a Demiurge who enters or reaches into it.

## Harvesting Essence

The Demiurge may reach into the Underreal to harvest Essence directly, bypassing the tick-by-tick Domain expression economy. This is fast and useful in emergencies, but:

- Almost universally disapproved of by Luminaries.
- Imposes a **Puissance malus** (see below).
- Carries the general risk inherent in any contact with the Underreal.

## Puissance Malus

Interacting with the Underreal imposes a temporary reduction to Puissance. This fades over time — the effect is a loan against future effectiveness, not permanent damage. The fading design is intentional: the Underreal is a resource under pressure, not a trap that punishes use forever.

**When you draw from the Underreal, the Underreal draws from you.**

Factors that may modulate recovery rate:
- Stronghold upgrades designed to guard against Underreal blowback (see below).
- High Silence or Void Domain expression in the universe — stillness and emptiness may accelerate recovery, while a high-Conflict, chaotic universe makes it harder to shed the Underreal's influence.

## Vestiges

When a Demiurge is cast into the Underreal as punishment (see Luminary Punishments below), they do not cease to exist entirely. Prolonged exposure to the Underreal's chaos degrades their connection to their former universe and fractures their coherence. The result is a **Vestige**: an insane, disconnected consciousness of a former Demiurge, still tethered to their home universe but unable to access or perceive it clearly.

Vestiges from *other* universes are not reachable — a Demiurge can only contact Vestiges who are predecessors of their own universe.

### Consulting a Vestige

The Demiurge may reach into the Underreal to attempt contact with a Vestige. The information available is uniquely relevant — no one else has managed this specific universe before — but the signal-to-noise ratio is poor by design. A Vestige cannot reliably distinguish memory from delusion, truth from fixation.

What the player receives is impressions and fragments, not clean answers. A Vestige's obsession with a particular region or Domain may reflect genuine historical significance, or may simply be the specific wound around which their madness crystallized. The player must interpret.

### The closed loop

The Demiurge knows that every Vestige was once what they are now — and that failure means becoming what they are consulting. A future Demiurge may one day reach in and find them.

## The Stronghold

The Demiurge can eventually construct a Stronghold: a demiplane outside the main universe where they can build upgrades and conduct certain actions with reduced Luminary visibility.

Relevant upgrades include:
- Mitigation of Puissance malus from Underreal interaction.
- Shielding certain actions from Luminary detection.

If discovered by Luminaries (directly or via Herald agents), access to the Stronghold is cut off entirely.

## Luminary Punishment Ladder

Luminaries respond to poor performance or rule violations on an escalating scale:

1. **Direct restrictions** — specific action types are blocked.
2. **Designation** — portions of the universe are declared off-limits; the Demiurge loses geographic access to them temporarily.
3. **Stronghold discovery** — if the Demiurge has built a Stronghold, Luminaries (or their Herald agents) may find it and cut off access, eliminating the hidden infrastructure.
4. **Casting out** — the Demiurge is expelled into the Underreal, becoming a Vestige. The universe continues under a new Demiurge.
